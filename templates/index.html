How to Use Monster Importer

Make sure your Roll20 subscription is at Pro tier

For Monster Importer to work, you have to have access to Mods (API Scripts). Monster Importer is a third-party Web App, so we do not have the legitimacy to change how Roll20 works or how it should. That said, unfortunately, you have to pay the higher price there to be able to user Monster Importer for its main purpose.

Install Monster Importer Extension to your browser

It will allow you to integrate Monster Importer to Roll20. Without it, you cannot import your favorite monsters to your player die for. After installing, close your browser and open again, to make sure it's working properly

Use our Mod Script in your campaign

Ok, you have the Pro Tier and the Extension, now it's time to Roll20 receive and understand what's going on. For that, we make an API Script that you can put on your Campaigns and use Monster Importer at it's fullest! That script will do the hard work for you and insert every aspect of your desired monsters into the game. Make sure to save it before running and refresh the Campaign page before trying to use it
