Do you think that creating a Monster Sheet in Roll20 is tedious?

Well, we thought in a solution to that!

Monster Importer is a WebApp that allow you to import any Monster from the SRD manuals or home brew created here to your Campaign in Roll20. This comes from the struggles to write every single trait, ability or action for every single monster or creature you want your players to face ingame.

Who is running Monster Importer
A madman for sure. I, madwarlock95, am the creator, developer and soul of Monster Importer. Until now, I received no active help to develop the WebApp, the extension or the Mod Script in Roll20. There's no honor in that. Some friends helped me in some things, like understanding the madness that is JavaScript.

Ok. So how much to create an account or use Monster Importer in my game?
No cost involved in this end. To create an account or use any Monster here, you just have to get a Pro subscription in Roll20 and download our extension. That's it! Monster Importer is completely free!

How can I use Monster Importer?
Follow this steps to use Monster Importer freely:
1. Get a Pro Subscription in Roll20
2. Install our extension
3. Put out Mod Script in the Mod(API) Scripts in your campaign in Roll20
That's it! Now you can use Monster Importer in your games!
